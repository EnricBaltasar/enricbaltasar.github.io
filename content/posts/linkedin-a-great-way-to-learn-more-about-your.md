A great way to learn more about your competitors is what I call translation-based research.

It all started when I was helping scaling a startup by opening a new market some years ago. SEO was one of the favourite channels, and the question was: Which content pieces should we push first?

The obvious answer was: We know what type of content drives most revenue in other markets, so let's use that internal knowledge. And some research later, we were leaking that information publicly -- to those who knew to read between lines.
